## 🔍 Overview

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance across regions, product categories, and customer segments. It is designed with a focus on clarity, structure, and ease of interpretation.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- **North region** leads overall sales performance (~$200K)  
- **Office Supplies** is the top-performing category (~$264K)  
- **January** records the highest monthly sales, indicating strong early-year demand  
- Sales show a **mid-year dip**, suggesting seasonal trends  
- Customer segments (Corporate, Home Office, Consumer) are relatively balanced
